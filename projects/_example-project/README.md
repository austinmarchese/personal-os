# Social Media Branding Campaign

*This is an example project to show the structure. Delete this and create your own.*

## Goal

Launch a consistent presence on LinkedIn and Twitter. Post 3x/week for 30 days. Measure: followers gained, engagement rate, DMs received.

## Timeline

- Start: March 1
- First checkpoint: March 15 (2 weeks)
- Complete: March 30

## Phases

1. **Setup** — Define content pillars, create templates, batch first week of posts
2. **Execute** — Post consistently, engage daily, track metrics
3. **Review** — Analyze what worked, document learnings, decide next steps

## Risks

- Running out of ideas → Batch 2 weeks of posts upfront
